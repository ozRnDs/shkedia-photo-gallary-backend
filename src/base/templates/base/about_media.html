Media's Life

The system breathes life into our MEDIA files and takes good care of them. While uploading the MEDIA, the system processes it. It creates a small copy as a thumbnail, ensuring alignment beforehand. We gather all the INFORMATION about the MEDIA. Then, each media gets its own special encryption key. That key is encrypted again and saved with the media's INFORMATION and thumbnail. The MEDIA itself is sent for safekeeping.

When we want to enjoy our MEDIA, the system carefully opens each and every little thumbnail. Then, it arranges them in collections according to our desires and sends them back to us, shining. Now, we can browse and search until we find that special MEDIA we want to print and save in a printed album. Until then, the MEDIA itself is safe from harm.
